About JiCken

MORE THAN WINGS

JiCken Wings & Cafe is Imus's boldest food destination — where crispy, saucy chicken wings meet artisan cafe drinks in one unforgettable experience. Tucked in Parklane Phase 4, Sabang Dasmarinas City, we've become the go-to hangout for barkadas, families, and food lovers who refuse to settle for ordinary.

We believe every meal should be an event. That's why we offer 12 signature flavors, unlimited promos designed for groups, a full cafe menu, and a vibe that keeps you coming back. From the first crispy bite to the last sip of your frappe — we make it count.
